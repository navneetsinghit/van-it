Driver-dashboard User Stories

- A driver can see their profile picture
- A driver can see their profile information
- A driver can activate their status
- A driver can de-activate their status

- If a job is available
	- A driver can see that a job is available
	- A driver can see a map that shows where the job will commence
	- A driver can accept the available job
	- A driver can decline the available job
- If a job is not available
	- A driver can see an "awaiting jobs" notification

- A driver can see their job history
- A driver can click on a job in their job history to see more details
